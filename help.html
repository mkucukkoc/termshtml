Welcome to Avenia Help. Find answers to common questions about our AI chat app.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start a chat in Avenia?

    Open the app, tap the "New Chat" button, and type your message. The AI will respond instantly.

  • How can I upload images or documents?

    In the chat input area, tap the "+" icon, select "Gallery" or "Documents", choose your file, and send. The AI can analyze and respond based on the upload.

  • What are premium features?

    Premium includes advanced AI models, unlimited chats, higher upload limits, and ad-free experience. Subscribe via the settings menu.

  • How do I subscribe to premium?

    Go to Settings > Subscription > Avenia Unlimited, and follow the payment prompts.

  • How do I cancel my subscription?

    Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription, or cancel through your app store account.

  • Is my data private?

    Yes, we prioritize privacy. See our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.

  • What if I encounter an error?

    Restart the app or check for updates. If issues persist, contact support at [email address].

  • Can I delete my chat history?

    Yes, go to the chat screen, tap the menu, and select "Delete Chat".
